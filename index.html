Photo Editing

Photo editing enhances and refines images, ensuring they communicate effectively. It involves adjusting various elements such as brightness, contrast, and color balance to improve overall appearance. Additionally, techniques like cropping, resizing, and retouching can be employed to achieve desired compositions and remove imperfections. Advanced editing may involve applying filters, overlays, and effects to evoke specific moods or styles. Ultimately, photo editing transforms raw images into polished, impactful visuals suitable for a variety of purposes, from personal memories to professional presentations.
